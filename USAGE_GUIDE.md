# How to Use the Ife BLIS GPA Calculator

Welcome! This guide will walk you through calculating your Grade Point Average (GPA) and Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) using this application.

## Step 1: Select Your Academic Level

-   **Part:** Choose your current academic year (e.g., Part 1, Part 2).
-   **Semester:** Select the semester for which you want to enter grades (e.g., Harmattan or Rain).

Once you make a selection, a list of courses for that semester will automatically appear.

## Step 2: Enter Your Grades

-   For each course listed, click the dropdown menu under the "Grade" column.
-   Select the grade you received for that course (A, B, C, D, E, or F).
-   If you didn't take a particular course, you can leave the grade as "--".

## Step 3: Add the Semester to Your Calculation

-   After entering all your grades for the semester, click the **"Add Semester"** button.
-   Your GPA for that semester and your overall CGPA will be instantly updated on the screen.
-   A summary of the semester you just added will appear at the bottom of the page.

You can repeat these steps to add as many semesters as you need. The calculator will keep track of everything for you.

## Step 4: View Your Final Results

-   Once you have added all your semesters, click the **"View My Results"** button.
-   This will take you to a detailed results page that shows a breakdown of your performance, your final CGPA, and other summary statistics.

## How to Start Over

If you want to clear all your entries and start a new calculation from scratch, simply click the **"Reset All"** button.

That's it! Enjoy using the calculator.
